Introduction

Technology has recently been replacing humans rapidly in our society. From cashiers being replaced by carts that scan your items at amazon fresh stores to Tesla's that will drive for you. People can now be replaced by lots of technology. Although this can be a bad thing and cause people to lose their jobs, this advance in technology is also revolutionary. New technology mixed with artificial intelligence is a combination that can help society immensly.one example of these two working together is how scientists from the Minnesota fish and wildlife department used drones with thermal cameras to track a population of whitetail deer. They used the cameras to locate fawns to capture and to show how efficient the drones were compared to people.
